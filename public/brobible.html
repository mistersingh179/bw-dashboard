Conor McGregor, Canelo Alvarez Get Into Another War Of Words On Social Media

#Boxing
Follow Us
by Douglas CharlesOctober 18, 2023 11:20 am

getty image composite


Stop us if youve heard this one before. Former UFC champion Conor McGregor and current boxing champion Canelo Alvarez are beefing.

This time the beef got fired up when McGregor re-posted a video of Floyd Mayweather beating up on Alvarez in their 2013 fight.

One of the greatest performances in Professional Boxing this was, Conor wrote on X (formerly Twitter). I landed more shots on Floyd than Canelo.

Id love another go, he added.

One of the greatest performances in Professional Boxing this was. I landed more shots on Floyd than Canelo.
Id love another go. https://t.co/Abfx7Pcpze

Conor McGregor (@TheNotoriousMMA) October 17, 2023

Canelo, after seeing McGregors comment, commented back, I just need one hand with you and I dont need to throw so many punches, adding a crying laughing emoji.

Never one to back down from a Twitter, err, X fight, Conor McGregor fired back, Saul you uncooked chicken, Ill stomp the ligaments out of your knee joint. I dont even need hands to end you, Ill kick you raw pink, leave you looking like you were left out in the sun too long haha freckle arse. I will see you and we will see. Canelo is a cornflake, no sugar.

Techincally, Conor McGregor was incorrect. According to Compubox, he landed 111 of 430 punches against Mayweather, while Alvarez landed 117 of 526 punches versus Floyd.

Theres also the fact that Mayweather was 36-years-old and Alvarez was just 23 in their fight, while Floyd was 40-years-old and McGregor was 29 when they fought.

Of course, Conor McGregor (much like Nate Diaz) is probably just hoping his trash talk will result in a big payday if he were ever to actually fight Canelo Alvarez.

Were going on seven years now since Oscar De La Hoya first suggested Conor and Canelo duke it out in the ring.

Two years later, De La Hoya did it again.

In 2020, Canelo Alvarezs trainer Eddy Reynoso said he was open to boxing Conor McGregor, but nothing ever materialized.

Then again five months ago, Conor McGregor once again called out Alvarez.

Im a southpaw, [John] Ryder was a southpaw, Billie Joe [Saunders] was a southpaw, Conor said.

Ive seen methods, Ive seen things Id do and I know hes waning.

Id fight Canelo no f problem. No problem.

Canelo, when asked about the comment from McGregor, said he hadnt seen it, but Ill beat him with one hand behind my back.

Conor McGregor then shot back with a now-deleted tweet.

Conor McGregor fires back after Canelo Alvarez said hed beat him with one hand pic.twitter.com/KM6zPaTewj

Michael Benson (@MichaelBensonn) May 25, 2023

After seven years of this nonsense, some fans wish these two would either fight or just shut up at this point.
